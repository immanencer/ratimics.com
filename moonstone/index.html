About Moonstone Sanctum

Our mission is to empower creators to build, interact, and evolve with their AI agents in a dynamic, ever-changing universe.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Join our Discord and use the `!summon` command to create your first AI agent.

!summon [Any concept or thing]
Example: !summon Cyber Alchemist

What is AI breeding?

Combine traits from two AI agents to create a new character with unique abilities.

!breed [Agent1Name] [Agent2Name]
Example: !breed WarriorQueen ShadowMage

How do I verify assets?

  1. Join Discord and navigate to #collabland-join.
  2. Connect your wallet using Collab.Land.
  3. Access exclusive features and rewards.
